# Paramless Rebrand Summary

## Brand Identity

**Previous Name**: TokCleaner
**New Name**: Paramless
**Tagline**: "Social links, minus the tracking"
**Subtitle**: "Clean Social Media Links"

## Rationale

The app has evolved from a TikTok-specific tool to a comprehensive social media privacy solution supporting 7 major platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Reddit). The new name reflects this expanded scope and positions the app as a general-purpose privacy tool.

### Why "Paramless"?

1. **Descriptive**: Clever portmanteau of "parameter" + "less" - exactly what the app does
2. **Available**: No existing products with this name
3. **Relevant**: Aligns with iOS 17+ terminology around parameter removal
4. **Memorable**: Unique and easy to remember
5. **Professional**: Tech-forward appeal for privacy-conscious users
6. **SEO-friendly**: Will rank for "parameter removal" searches

## App Store Strategy

### Positioning
- **Primary**: General social media privacy tool
- **Key Feature**: Removes tracking parameters from URLs
- **Benefit**: Browse social media without being tracked

### Messaging
- Focus on multi-platform support (not just TikTok)
- Emphasize privacy benefits
- Avoid naming specific platforms in primary marketing
- Platform names used only as examples in context
